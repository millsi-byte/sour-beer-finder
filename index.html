      Your cities

      S4S re-reads every known tap list about every 4 hours, and gives each covered city a full overnight re-scan for brand-new tap-list sources once a week — nothing is fetched on the fly. Add the cities you drink in (or plan to visit), and tap ★ to set your home city for one-tap access.

        Areas with live tap data

        Cities outside these areas still work — you’ll see every brewery nearby, just without the 🍋 live sour data until the area joins the overnight scan. Want yours added? Message @search4sourbeer.

        Missing a brewery?

        The brewery list comes from a public database that misses some spots (even famous ones). Report one and it joins the app and the scans.
